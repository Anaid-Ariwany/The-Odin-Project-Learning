# 1. Understanding the Problem
- Creating a rock, paper, scissors game program that takes the user's input and the computer's random input, compares them, decides a winner, and awards the winner points based on the rules of the game. 
- There are only 3 valid inputs: rock, paper, scissors
- The rules are:
    1. Rock crushes Scissors
    2. Scissors cuts paper
    3. Paper covers Rock
- The possible choices:
    1. Rock & Scissors: Rock wins
    2. Rock & Paper: Paper wins
    3. Scissors & Paper: Scissors wins
